About the Project

Meet the Team

A second-year BSc (Hons) Computer Science group project at the University of Westminster. We built AmbuNow to make ambulance booking faster, clearer, and more reliable in critical moments.

Our Objective

Anyone can book an ambulance easily in critical conditions.

  • Fast communication between the user and the provider.
  • Show the closest hospital and service options.
  • Share incident reports in advance so patients arrive more stabilized.
