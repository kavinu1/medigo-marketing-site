About the Project

Meet the Team

A second-year BSc (Hons) Computer Science group project at the University of Westminster. MediGo is designed to support hospitals with real-time ambulance coordination, faster dispatch decisions, and secure communication between operations staff and drivers.

Our Objective

Enable hospitals to coordinate ambulances efficiently and safely.

Real-time Assignment

Task assignment from hospital dispatchers with instant notification

Secure Tracking

Location tracking and status reporting during trips

Operational Awareness

Support visibility without transmitting patient records
