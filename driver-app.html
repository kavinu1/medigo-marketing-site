About this app

Reviewed by MediGo Product Team

MediGo Driver App helps ambulance drivers receive assignments fast, navigate to pickup locations, and share live trip status with hospitals. It is designed for daily field use with a simple interface and low-friction workflow.

Key features

  • Live assignment alerts: Get immediate notifications for new dispatch requests.
  • Integrated navigation: Open destination routes quickly to reduce response delays.
  • Trip status updates: Share en route, arrived, and completed status in real time.
  • Driver-focused UI: Large actions and clear screens for field use.
  • Release-based updates: New builds are published on this page with history.

Trust and safety

The app is built for operational reliability and clear communication between drivers and dispatch teams. Drivers can verify assignment details before confirming actions, helping reduce route confusion and handling errors.
