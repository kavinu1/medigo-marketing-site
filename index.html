Overview & Objective

A coordinated way for hospitals to manage emergency ambulance fleets.

  • Assign emergency transfers digitally instead of calling drivers.
  • Track vehicle movement to support informed routing decisions.
  • Monitor trip status and driver availability.

Problems & Challenges

Operational gaps observed in Sri Lankan emergency coordination.

  • Hospital-to-ambulance communication often relies on calls.
  • No central dashboard for tracking ambulances in motion.
  • Trip documentation and logs remain manual or fragmented.

Features

Designed for operational efficiency, transparency, and safety.

Instant Dispatch Alerts

Hospitals assign a transfer, and the driver's device receives an immediate notification.

Automated Trip Records

Logs capture timestamps, vehicle status, and assignment details for audit.

Service Categorisation

Vehicles can be tagged (e.g., general transfer, critical, equipment transport).

Availability Filters

View ambulances by distance, availability, or assignment status.

GPS Monitoring

Supports routing efficiency and helps hospitals prepare in advance.

Feature Details

How digital coordination supports clinical operations.

Instant Dispatch Alerts
  • Allocations are issued digitally from the dashboard.
  • Drivers accept and acknowledge through their mobile devices.
Service Categorisation
  • Helps assign the correct transport type for each operational scenario.
  • Supports coordination between public and private fleets.
Availability Filters
  • Spot closest or idle vehicles.
  • Prioritise assignment efficiency.
GPS Monitoring
  • Shows travel progress and arrival proximity.
  • Allows hospitals to prepare ahead of arrival.

FAQs

General clarification about the system.

Is MediGo a clinical emergency service?
No — MediGo is a coordination and tracking system. Clinical emergencies must still be escalated through appropriate emergency channels.
Does MediGo handle patient records?
No — the system does not store clinical patient information.
Where is tracking visible?
Only authorised hospital staff assigned to fleet coordination can view location data.
