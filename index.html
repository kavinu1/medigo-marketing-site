Overview & Objective

Anyone can book an ambulance easily in critical conditions.

  • Fast communication between the user and the provider.
  • Users can see which hospital and service is closest to them.
  • Patients arrive more stabilized since the incident report is shared in advance.

Problems & Challenges

Real pain points we’re solving in Sri Lanka.

  • In Sri Lanka, ambulance booking can be a major challenge for many people.
  • Explaining the exact location of the incident can be time‑consuming.
  • Waiting without being able to check ambulance location is stressful.

Feature details

How each capability improves outcomes during emergencies.

Tracking capability
  • Patients can track the current location of the ambulance.
  • The route with the smallest ETA is shown on the map.
  • Helps the patient and family prepare for the ambulance’s arrival.
Filter by proximity, price, availability
  • Check real‑time availability and see the nearest options.
  • Sort service providers by price when cost matters.
Choosing the type of service
  • Select public or private services as needed.
  • If public, details are sent to the nearest government hospital.
  • If private, pick a provider based on proximity and price.
Incident priority checking
  • Assess severity to prioritize high‑risk incidents.
  • Include a doctor with the ambulance for critical cases.
Detailed incident report
  • Key details (time, location, vitals, initial care) are captured and shared with the ER.
  • Hospitals prepare resources in advance, improving stabilization upon arrival.

FAQs

Answers to common questions.

Is AmbuNow an emergency service?
AmbuNow connects you to nearby verified providers. In life‑threatening emergencies, always call your local emergency number first.
How fast can an ambulance arrive?
Arrival time varies by location and traffic. Our current median is 6–8 minutes in covered cities.
How is my data protected?
We use encryption in transit and at rest. Only necessary data is shared with providers to deliver care.
